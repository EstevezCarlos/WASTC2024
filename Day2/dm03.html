Different raster graphic formats

Format Upsides Downsides
JPG/JPEG - Good for photos
- Very small size due to lossy compression 		- Data/quality loss
- No transparency (optional)
PNG - Lossles compession
- Transparency (optional) 		- Larger files due to lossless compression
WEBP - Does support both types of compression
- Smalest files for given quality
- Does support transparency
- Support animation 		- Not every graphic program support it
